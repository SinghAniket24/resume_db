12-08-2004

Times of India

Article 1: Paris Olympics: Six medals and six near misses; India finishes 71st in medal tally

Neeraj Chopra, Manu Bhaker, Sarabjot Singh, Swapnil Kusale, Aman Sehrawat and the Indian hockey team contributed to six medals, including a silver and five bronze, and gave reasons to celebrate. India managed the 71st place in the medals tally.

Article 2: Hindenburg Research Report Highlights: SEBI advises caution on Hindenburg report

Hindenburg Research released a report alleging market watchdog SEBI chairperson Madhabi Buch's involvement in Adani Group's offshore funds, which were allegedly used in a "money siphoning scandal."

Article 3: Financial strain of disability in India high, insurance aims to bridge gap

Many Indians face financial hardship due to disability. Insurance companies are stepping forward with innovative solutions to address this critical gap in financial security.

Article 4: Maldives a key ally of India in Indian Ocean region, says EAM Jaishankar

The Maldives is a key partner for India in the Indian Ocean region. Jaishankar's remarks came during the inauguration of projects facilitated by the Government of India.
